Welcome to Cambridge Sword Fencing Club

Cambridge Sword is a dynamic and successful club for young people aged 5 to 18 and we have a local, regional, and growing national reputation for producing active, successful fencers at club and international level.

Whether you are a beginner or more experienced fencer, there’s a fencing session to suit you at Cambridge Sword; after school, evenings and at weekends. We also organise individual lessons for intermediate and advanced fencers wishing to develop faster and get into deeper tactical and technical scenarios.

We have holiday activity camps for beginners to advanced fencers.
